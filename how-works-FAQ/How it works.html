Learn How to Play?

Step 1: Get Started

Select the match you want to predict and click on predict now before the timer runs out

Step 2: Make Predictions

Predict your answers to the questions for each match, and lock your predictions

Step 3: View Results

Navigate to Pepsi Cricket Fever Homepage & Click on Completed button for recent predictions

Step 4: Rewards

You get right answers to lead the leaderboard and win exciting prizes
